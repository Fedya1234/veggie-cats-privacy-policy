Veggie Cats — Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 25, 2026

If you would like to request deletion of your personal data associated with Veggie Cats, please contact us using the email address below.

How to request data deletion

  1. Send an email to fedyadanilov@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line: Data Deletion Request – Veggie Cats.
  3. In your message, please include any details that help us identify your request, such as your Facebook account ID, device ID, or other relevant information associated with your use of the game.

What happens next

After receiving your request, we will review it and delete or anonymize the personal data we are able to identify, unless we are required to retain certain information for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Processing time

We aim to respond to verified requests within a reasonable time.
